Founded in 1994, Baba Farid Group of Institutions (BFGI) is a prominent education hub in India under the leadership of Dr. Gurmeet Singh Dhaliwal. Focused on academic excellence, BFGI collaborates with major companies like Microsoft, Oracle, SAP, HP, HCL, Nokia, and affiliations with industrial bodies. Emphasizing research, employability, and entrepreneurship, BFGI consistently excels in academics, sports, and extracurriculars. This commitment to holistic education positions BFGI as a leader in shaping student success.
